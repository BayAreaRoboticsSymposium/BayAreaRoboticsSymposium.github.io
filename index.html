About

The 2026 Bay Area Robotics Symposium aims to bring together roboticists from the Bay Area. The program will consist of a mix of faculty, student and industry presentations. For more details, see the event schedule.

Faculty Organizers: 

Negar Mehr (UC Berkeley), Mark Mueller (UC Berkeley), and Monroe D. Kennedy III (Stanford)

Advisory Committee:
