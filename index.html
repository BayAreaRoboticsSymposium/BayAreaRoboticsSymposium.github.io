About

The 2025 Bay Area Robotics Symposium aims to bring together roboticists from the Bay Area. The program will consist of a mix of faculty, student and industry presentations. For more details, see the event schedule.

Faculty Organizers: 

Grace Gao (Stanford), Negar Mehr (UC Berkeley), and Mark Mueller (UC Berkeley)

Venue

The event will be held on the campus of Stanford University at the David and Joan Traitel Building of the Hoover Institution.

Available parking around the venue is shown below: the red "S" marker is the venue, and the yellow "P" square markers show nearby visitor parking garages. More details on visitor parking found here.

The Open House after the main programming will be held at the Stanford Robotics Center (SRC) in the basement of the David Packard Electrical Engineering Building.

Code of conduct

We expect all attendees (whether they are affiliated with Stanford University or not) to adhere to the University's code of conduct. Any concerns about participant conduct may be reported directly to the organizers, or staff at the welcome desk.
